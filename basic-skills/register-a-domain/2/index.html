Register a Domain


Part 2 - Select Your Domain



Once you sign into Freenom, click Services on the navigation menu and click Register a New Domain on the sub-menu that appears.


Now you will be able to type in what you want your domain name to be. A domain name is what comes before the period in the URL. In bradykondek.ga, the domain name would be bradykondek. Once you type in your desired domain name, click the big rounded blue circle that says Check Availability.


A list of available domains for you to choose will then load up after clicking the button. If you want a free domain, choose from the top part of the list that says free domains. Once you find the domain that you want that is available, to the right of that domain click the blue button that says Get it now!. When it turns into a green button saying Selected!, click the green button towards the top right that says Checkout.
